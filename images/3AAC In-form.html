3AAC In-form

Use this form to highlight ANY event, error, hazard, near-miss or concern which you believe did or could effect safety. Examples include (but are not limited to) inaccurate or confusing procedures, lack of correct equipment, local procedures not following those 'in the book', 'I didn't have .....', 'they did ...', 'I nearly did....'. Please give a complete story of the situation. One way to include all the important facts in your report is to make sure you have answered the following quesitons:

  1. What happened (or nearly happend)?
  2. Why did it happen?
  3. What did you do about it?
  4. Are there any recommendations you would make to prevent reoccurance?
